Project Viability

"Should we build it?"

NPV | IRR | BCR | PPP

Project Viability

  • NPV > 0 = Accept project
  • IRR > required rate = Accept
  • BCR > 1 = Benefits exceed costs
  • Payback Period = Shorter is better
  • Financial viability = Will investors profit?
  • Economic viability = Will society benefit?
  • When financial ↓ but economic ↑ → Govt steps in via PPP
  • Feasibility = Technical + Financial + Economic + Environmental + Social
  • Vizhinjam: NPV negative without govt grant

Risk Management

"What can go wrong?"

PFCEO | Matrix | EMV | ATMA

Risk Management

  • Process: Identify → Assess → Mitigate → Monitor
  • PFCEO risks: Political, Financial, Construction, Environmental, Operational
  • Risk Matrix: Probability x Impact (1-5 each)
  • EMV = Probability x Impact (in Rs.)
  • ATMA response: Avoid, Transfer, Mitigate, Accept
  • Risk Allocation: Give risk to party best able to manage
  • Construction risk → Private | Political → Govt
  • Monte Carlo: 1000s of random scenarios → probability distribution
  • Sensitivity: Change 1 variable → see NPV change

Asset Management

"How to maintain what we built?"

ISO 55000 | Lifecycle | Valuation

Asset Management

  • ISO 55000: Align assets with org objectives
  • Lifecycle: Plan → Acquire → Operate → Maintain → Dispose
  • Historical Cost: What you paid (books)
  • Replacement Cost: Cost to replace today (insurance)
  • Market Value: What someone would pay (selling)
  • DRC: Replacement cost minus wear (fair value)
  • Maintenance: Reactive → Preventive → Condition-Based → Predictive
  • Condition Assessment: Rate health 1-5, drives priority
  • AM Plan: Inventory + Condition + Strategy + Finance + Risk

Smart Infrastructure

"How to optimize with tech?"

IoT | Digital Twin | BIM | AI

Smart Infrastructure

  • IoT: Sensors collecting real-time data
  • Digital Twin: Virtual replica of physical asset
  • BIM: 3D model for design + construction
  • AI/ML: Predictive maintenance, demand forecast
  • Smart Transport: GPS, adaptive signals, EV
  • Smart Grid: Smart meters, renewable integration
  • Smart Water: Leak detection, quality monitoring
  • Cybersecurity: Critical - connected = vulnerable
  • Smart → better Asset Mgmt, Risk Mgmt, Viability decisions

Formula Sheet (20 Marks Numericals)

NPV (Net Present Value)

NPV = -I + CF1/(1+r)^1 + CF2/(1+r)^2 + ... + CFn/(1+r)^n
NPV > 0 = ACCEPT | NPV < 0 = REJECT
I=10000, CF=3000/yr, 5yrs, r=10%
= -10000 + 2727 + 2479 + 2254 + 2050 + 1863
= +1,373 → ACCEPT

IRR (Internal Rate of Return)

IRR = r1 + [NPV1/(NPV1-NPV2)] x (r2-r1)

Find rate where NPV = 0
IRR > required rate = ACCEPT
NPV at 15% = +500, NPV at 20% = -200
IRR = 15 + [500/700] x 5 = 18.57%

BCR (Benefit-Cost Ratio)

BCR = PV of Benefits / PV of Costs
BCR > 1 = ACCEPT | Used for govt/social projects
PV Benefits=15000, PV Costs=12000
BCR = 15000/12000 = 1.25 → ACCEPT

Payback Period

Simple: Payback = Investment / Annual CF

Unequal: Cumulate CFs until recovered
Shorter payback = Better (but ignores time value)
I=10000, Annual CF=2500
Payback = 10000/2500 = 4 years

EMV (Expected Monetary Value)

EMV = Probability x Impact (in Rs.)
Higher EMV = More critical risk
Risk A: 30% x 10L = 3L
Risk B: 10% x 50L = 5L
→ Risk B more critical (higher EMV)

Risk Score (Matrix)

Score = Likelihood(1-5) x Consequence(1-5)
1-4: Low | 5-9: Med | 10-15: High | 16-25: Critical
Prob=4, Impact=5 → Score=20 CRITICAL
Prob=2, Impact=3 → Score=6 MEDIUM

Depreciation (Straight Line)

Dep/yr = (Cost - Salvage) / Useful Life

DRC = Replacement Cost x (Remaining/Total Life)
Used for asset valuation questions
Cost=50000, Salvage=5000, Life=10yr
Dep = 4500/yr
Book Value after 6yr = 50000-(4500x6) = 23000
DRC: Replace=80000, 4yr left → 80000x4/10 = 32000

Present Value & Annuity

PV = FV / (1+r)^n

PV Annuity = CF x [(1-(1+r)^-n) / r]
Building block for NPV, BCR calculations
10000 after 3yr at 8%:
PV = 10000/1.08^3 = 7,938

5000/yr for 5yr at 10%:
= 5000 x 3.791 = 18,955

Exam Strategy

Theory (20m)

Write definitions briefly, then DRAW DIAGRAMS. Risk matrix, asset lifecycle, PPP spectrum. Visual answers score more. Use the 4-pillar framework for any general question.

Case Study (20m)

Format: 2-line context → identify theory → apply framework → specific examples from case → lessons. Compare Dabhol vs Airport for PPP questions. Always mention risk allocation.

Numericals (20m)

Write formula FIRST. Substitute values. Show EVERY step. State decision rule. Give conclusion. Method marks > arithmetic. Even wrong numbers with right method = marks.
